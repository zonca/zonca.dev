CloudBank provides a simplified way for researchers and educators to access commercial cloud resources, including Amazon Web Services (AWS) and Google Cloud.

It is particularly useful for small-scale research production infrastructure and AI workloads.

Learn more at: [https://www.cloudbank.org/request-access-commercial-cloud-resources](https://www.cloudbank.org/request-access-commercial-cloud-resources)
